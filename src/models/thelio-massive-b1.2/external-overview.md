## Back side overview:

### Audio Port Guide:

- Green: Line Out / Front Speaker Out
- Pink: Mic In
- Light Blue: Line In / Side Speaker Out
- Orange: Center/Subwoofer Out
- Black: Rear Speaker Out
- White (square): S/PDIF Digital Out

### Power Switch Guide:

- 1: On
- 0: Off
